Life Insurance

Life insurance offers financial protection for families upon the policyholder's death, covering expenses like mortgages and education. It comes in different types, including term, whole, and universal life, tailored to diverse needs. Premiums are determined by factors such as age and health. Additionally, life insurance can serve as an investment, accruing cash value over time for borrowing or withdrawals.
